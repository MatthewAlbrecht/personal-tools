Genius Annotation

The title track for HYPERYOUTH is the perfect encapsulation of being afraid to grow up. As children, we often say I never want to grow up and adults, we wish we never did. Joey Valence & Brae launch headfirst with the single for the album HYPERYOUTH to push forward that message.

First teased on several billboards and a post to their socials, fans were treated after their collaboration with JPEGMAFIA and the success that was NO HANDS.
